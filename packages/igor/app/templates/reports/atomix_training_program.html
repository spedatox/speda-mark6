Progression

  • Double progression. Every set at the top of its rep range at the prescribed RPE → add 2.5 kg upper, 5 kg lower.
  • Missed session. One is dropped, never rescheduled. Two in a week rolls the block forward by one week.
  • Stall. Two flat weeks on a lift → reset the load by 10 % and rebuild across three weeks.
